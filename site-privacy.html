Website Privacy & Cookies

This notice covers the vilaverdeapps website only. It does not cover our iOS apps — each app has its own privacy policy (Snap-Back, Patter).

Last updated: 22 June 2026.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use this website — for example, which pages are viewed and roughly where traffic comes from. This helps us improve the site. IP addresses are anonymized.

Google Analytics sets cookies and sends usage data to Google, which acts as our data processor. See the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites that use its services.

Your consent

No analytics cookies are set and no data is sent to Google unless you accept. On your first visit a banner asks you to Accept or Decline. We remember your choice in your browser so the banner does not reappear.

Change your mind? To withdraw consent, clear this site's data (cookies and local storage) in your browser settings. The banner will appear again on your next visit, letting you choose afresh. Declining leaves no analytics cookies and sends nothing to Google.

Cookies we use

  • Consent preference — a small value stored in your browser's local storage to remember whether you accepted or declined analytics. Set by this site, not shared with anyone.
  • Google Analytics cookies (e.g. _ga, _ga_*) — set only after you accept, used to distinguish visitors and sessions.

Contact

Questions about this website's privacy practices? Reach us via GitHub.
