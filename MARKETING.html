Snap-Back: Space Mining

The tactile satisfaction of snapping a rubber band combined with the chaotic physics of a high-speed slingshot. Easy to pick up, impossible to put down.

Download on the App Store How it works

How it works

Touch and drag anywhere on screen to aim and build power — a predictive dotted line shows your trajectory. Release to launch. Upon impact with a geometric ore target, an elastic tether automatically attaches. Then snap back to the Core (your center base). The further you traveled, the bigger the score.

Highlights

  • Slingshot launch — touch and drag to aim; a predictive dotted trajectory shows where you'll go.
  • Elastic tether grapple — hit a target and a tether auto-attaches; snap back to base to score.
  • Multi-grapple — daisy-chain multiple targets for a score multiplier, at increasingly chaotic risk.
  • Taptic Engine — stretching vibration as you pull back, a sharp thud on impact, and a shattered pulse if the line breaks.
  • Ghost Runs — a translucent replay of your Game Center friends' best rounds plays in the background.
  • Daily Trickshot — one procedurally generated level refreshes every 24 hours; every player in the world competes on the same layout.
  • Customization — unlock tether skins (Neon Lightning, Chrome Chain) and Core Hub designs via gameplay achievements.
  • No account — nothing to sign up for; your progress stays on your device.
